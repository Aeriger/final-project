Animation is essentially the stringing together of several images in order to create the illusion of movement. Everything we see on our computers and phones is actually an extension of animation; the movement we see on displays is actually made up of several images, called frames. This is just one example of the versatility of animation, which is what makes the realm of animated media so compelling. Whether it be films or video games, animation is only limited by imagination (and perhaps computer power as well), allowing for the creation of several interactive and beautiful works that can’t be replicated by live action films.
